# Ovation — Product Overview

Ovation is a social app for live performance (theatre, stand-up, comedy) in Paris — the "Letterboxd for the stage."

- Users log shows they've seen, rate them, write reviews, follow other users, discover new shows.
- Two products: mobile app (this repo) + an editorial Instagram account (out of scope for this repo).
- Target launch: September 2026, iOS + Android.
- Catalogue: 500 Parisian shows at launch.
